Peanut Butter And Jelly

  • Time: 2 minutes if you're fast enough
  • Serves: 2 little humans
Ingredients Quantity
Peanut Butter | 1 Spoonful
Bread | 2 Slices
Jelly (Or SubstituteFor Honey) | 1 Spoonful (Two If You're Sad)

blank

Step 1: Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread.

Step 2: Spread jelly on other slice of bread.

Step 3: Put the two sticky sides together unless you want to get messy.

Step 4: Cut diagonally with knife. (This Step Is Required).

Pizzadilla

  • Time: No more than 10min unless you do it wrong
  • Serves: 1 sad soul
Ingredients Quantity
Tortillas | 2
Butter | Just enough
Cheese (Colby jack is best) | 1 handful

blank

Step 1: heat griddle to 325 degrees.

Step 2: Spread butter on one side of each tortilla.

Step 3: Lay one tortilla butter-side down on the griddle and add cheese and pepperoni. (Do NOT add the other tortilla yet)

Step 4: When tortilla is golden, add the other tortilla butter-side up and flip.

Step 5: When all the cheese is melted and the tortillas are golden, take the pizzadilla off the griddle.

Step 6: Cut into 8 pieces. First Come First Serve

Popcorn

  • Time: 2:30 minutes
  • Serves: Not enough
Ingredients Quantity
Bag of microwaveable popcorn | 1
Butter | Enough to drown out the lonliness

blank

Step 1: Put the bag of popcorn in the microwave and set for 2:30 minutes.

Step 2: Take the bag out when popping slows or time runs out.

Step 3: Melt the butter and pour over popcorn.
